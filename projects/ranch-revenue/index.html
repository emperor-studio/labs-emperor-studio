Key Insight

Santa Clarita's city-wide production decline (−17.9% permits, −27.4% shoot days Q1 over Q1) confirms the industry-wide slowdown is real. However, Blue Cloud Ranch's revenue decline of −45% in 2025 significantly outpaces the market average — suggesting BCR faces both a market headwind and a competitive positioning challenge that requires active outreach to reverse.
