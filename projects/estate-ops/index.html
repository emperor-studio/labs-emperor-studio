Core idea

Dylan’s dedicated Mac Studio becomes the digital concierge desk and operations brain. The Personal Assistant role becomes Dylan’s digital extension, while the House Manager role becomes the estate’s operational extension.

Why this is better

  • The Mac Studio becomes the stable long-term system.
  • The current iPhone can begin operating as the Personal Assistant role immediately.
  • The House Manager role remains separate and can be added cleanly with its own device next.
  • Coordination relationships are explicit instead of implied.

Main rule

The Mac Studio is the control plane. The iPhones are the execution layer.

Routing

What routes through the Mac Studio vs the iPhones

Best on the Mac Studio

  • Dashboards and control views
  • Shared calendars, notes, docs, and summaries
  • Password manager administration
  • Automation, reminders, routing, and reporting
  • Long-term memory of what happened and why

Best on the role iPhones

  • Apps that open, unlock, or locally control physical systems
  • Bluetooth, Wi‑Fi, NFC, HomeKit, and local-presence flows
  • SMS and 2FA flows tied to the role
  • Portable communications while in the field

Careful split

  • Email identities can be role-based, but summaries and admin visibility should flow back to the Mac Studio.
  • Each role should have its own dedicated phone number, and that number should either live on the role iPhone or be easily forwarded to the current responsible human or backup device while contact logs and escalation notes are captured centrally.
  • Credentials should be managed through a proper vault, not only stored on the devices themselves.
  • Role phone numbers should be treated as permanent service numbers, not personal numbers, so they remain stable even when the human in the role changes.

Handoff and security

How to keep the system stable as people change

Handoff checklist

  • Recover the physical role iPhone.
  • Change the device passcode.
  • Review the Apple account trusted devices and recovery options.
  • Remove the prior human from password manager access.
  • Rotate sensitive passwords if needed.
  • Review 2FA enrollments, vendor sessions, and Home/security app permissions.
  • Update the Mac Studio admin record showing who currently holds the role.
  • Brief the new human using the role handbook and escalation notes.

Key principle

The system must survive staff turnover gracefully.

Future layer

Where Home Assistant fits

Why add Home Assistant later

  • Centralize property systems into a cleaner control layer
  • Reduce app sprawl for routine estate operations
  • Create better dashboards and automations
  • Give Dylan curated visibility without needing every vendor app personally

Important caution

  • Do not try to replace every vendor app immediately
  • Keep role iPhones for mobile control, local presence, and edge-case apps
  • Let Home Assistant become the unifying layer gradually
  • Use the Mac Studio as the natural host and control center for it

Recommended next move

Build Dylan’s Mac Studio as the central operations hub first, use the existing iPhone as the Personal Assistant device now, then add the House Manager iPhone next. That gives you the cleanest path to a truly seamless private-office system.
