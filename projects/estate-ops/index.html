System logic

What routes through the Mac Studio vs the role iPhones

  • Best on the Mac Studio: dashboards, shared calendars, notes, docs, summaries, automation, password administration, and long-term continuity
  • Best on the role iPhones: apps that unlock, open, or locally control physical systems, plus SMS/2FA and field communication
  • Email identities can be role-based, but summaries and admin visibility should flow back to the Mac Studio
  • Role phone numbers should be treated as permanent service numbers, not personal numbers

Handoff and continuity

How the system survives staff change

  • Recover the physical role iPhone
  • Change the device passcode
  • Review trusted devices and recovery options
  • Remove the prior human from vault access
  • Rotate sensitive passwords when needed
  • Update the Mac Studio admin record showing who currently holds the role
  • Brief the new human using role handbooks and escalation notes
