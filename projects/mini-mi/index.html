Mini Mi · My Intelligence

A personal system for a life with less friction.

Mini Mi is a bespoke white-glove service for people who value time, privacy, and calm. We help you design the assistant you have always wished existed, then shape it into a living system that supports the way you actually move through the world.

Maximize efficiency. Minimize input.

What it is

Everyone has small points of resistance: the follow-up that slips, the note that lives in the wrong place, the decision that gets made over and over, the errand that waits too long, the preference that has to be explained again.

Mini Mi begins there. We listen closely, map the recurring pain points, and build a personal support system around the things that quietly cost time, energy, and attention.

Our process

Deliberate by design.

01

Listen

We learn how your life actually works: what matters, what repeats, what frustrates you, and what would feel lighter if it were handled well.

02

Map

We turn scattered needs into a clear picture: reminders, preferences, routines, communication habits, household needs, projects, and decisions.

03

Build

We create a system around you, not around a template. The goal is practical support that feels natural, respectful, and easy to use.

04

Refine

As your life changes, the system is adjusted. The work is not finished when it is installed; it improves as it learns where the friction lives.

For whom

For people who understand the value of time.

Mini Mi is for clients who do not want another thing to manage. They want fewer dropped balls, fewer repeated explanations, fewer avoidable delays, and more confidence that important details are being carried forward.

It is for people willing to invest the time up front to create a system that gives time back every week after.
