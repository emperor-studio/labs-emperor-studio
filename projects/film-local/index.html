The Data

The decline is real.
And it's accelerating.

Blue Cloud Ranch — a 250-acre production location in Santa Clarita — generated $4.65M in 2022. By 2025, that number had collapsed to $847K. An 82% decline in three years. The LA wildfires, the WGA/SAG strike, and the industry-wide slowdown have compounded into a crisis that threatens not just locations — but the entire ecosystem that supports them.

This isn't an isolated story. Santa Clarita saw a 27% decline in film shoot days from Q1 2025 to Q1 2026. The pattern is clear. The question is whether we act now or watch production leave permanently.

Our Mission

Make Los Angeles the easiest place
in the world to shoot.

Film LoCAL is a California-focused initiative to keep production local through smarter policy, stronger community relationships, and real economic advocacy. We believe Los Angeles doesn't just have a claim to being the center of the film industry — it has an obligation to fight for it.

📋

Permitting Reform

Streamline approvals, reduce red tape, and cut the costs that make productions choose Atlanta, Albuquerque, and Vancouver over LA.

💰

Competitive Incentives

California's tax incentive program must keep pace with Georgia, New Jersey, and international competition. We advocate for policy that makes CA the obvious financial choice.

🤝

Industry Coalition

Location owners, crew, vendors, and studios fighting with one voice. Production thriving in LA benefits everyone — from craft services to local restaurants to schools.

Who We Are

Rooted in California.
Backed by real data.
