The Sunday Decision — 3 Doors
Promote
This is real.
Next week deepens this app. No new app starts until this one has legs or is archived.
Triggered by: real user pull, or hitting the durable threshold.
Continue
One more week.
Maximum one extension. The magic moment works but distribution needs attention.
Triggered by: clear signal, specific gap.
⚠ The danger door. No extensions beyond Week 2.
Archive
Done. Learned.
Lessons captured. Hosting cancelled. Repo archived. Most weeks end here — that's correct, not failure.
Default door. Use it freely.

Mental Model

This is a search algorithm, not a production line.

The goal isn't 52 products. It's finding the 2–3 that deserve to stop being weekly and become real. The cadence is how you search fast enough to actually find them. When something breaks through — stop the cadence and go deep. That's the whole point.

Open Questions — Decisions Needed Before Week 1
01
When does Week 1 start?
Pick a Monday. Week Zero is for building the starter template and seeding the idea backlog.
02
What does "durable" mean?
Define the threshold that promotes an app out of the cadence before you start. Options: 10 weekly active non-team users for 3 weeks · 1 paying user · personal daily use for 3 weeks.
03
Where does each app get announced?
One primary launch channel per app type. Twitter thread? HN? Product Hunt? Reddit niche sub? Agree before Week 1 so launch isn't improvised.
04
Domain and hosting strategy?
One root with subpaths (apps.bluecloud.com/appname) or separate root domains per app? Affects cost, branding, and how seriously each app gets treated.
05
What categories do you care about?
Film/media tools · ranch/location ops · entertainment · productivity · creative tools · something else? Knowing this seeds the backlog with ideas that actually fit your world.
