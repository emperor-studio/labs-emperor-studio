About this prototype

labs concept · v0.1

What this is. A Labs concept page showing what a private insurance control panel could look like once an insurance portfolio is extracted, normalized, and mapped. All data on this page is mock.

What it solves. Insurance portfolios fragment over time. Different brokers, different renewal dates, different carriers, overlapping riders, buried exclusions. The question "what am I actually covered for?" becomes a phone call instead of a glance.

What Dylan would upload. Declarations pages, full policies, endorsements, broker invoices, renewal notices. PDF in, structured data out. Every extracted field shows a confidence score; anything below 85% flags for human review.

What Dylan would get back. One coverage map across every asset and entity. A renewal timeline with decision windows. A gap + overlap report with questions already written for the broker call. Scenario modeling for consolidations and limit changes.

Guardrails. Decision support, not legal or insurance advice. Local-first document storage. Nothing leaves the machine without explicit permission. Human-in-the-loop for every extracted field and every scenario estimate.
