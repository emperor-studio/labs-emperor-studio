Mini Mi · concept variant

Stop renting your continuity.

A white-glove personal intelligence system for people whose life, work, notes, messages, reminders, and half-finished ideas are scattered across too many apps and too many tabs. Installed on dedicated hardware. Tuned to one human. Built to stay with you.

Core frame

Not another AI app. A private system with memory, messaging, specialists, and continuity.

Dedicated Mac mini Named assistant Private memory Specialist agents Ongoing stewardship

The problem

You don't need more AI. You need a system.

Your life is spread across email, messages, notes, documents, saved links, voice notes, calendars, tasks, screenshots, and half-started plans. You keep restating the same things to different software. Most AI is powerful for a moment and forgetful by default. Nothing actually keeps continuity for you.

What breaks

  • Important context gets lost
  • Decisions live in the wrong channel
  • Ideas are repeated instead of compounded
  • Admin overhead keeps growing
  • AI helps, but does not stay with you

02

03

Optional focused agents for business ideas, operations, philosophy, language learning, project support, or household roles.

Ongoing tuning

Model routing, memory hygiene, integrations, dashboards, and workflows adjusted as the system becomes more deeply useful.

What it can hold

  • Calendar, reminders, and personal operational continuity
  • Messages, summaries, follow-up, and coordination
  • Writing, planning, research, and project support
  • Memory that persists across time instead of resetting every session
  • Private dashboards and control surfaces
  • Specialists for focused domains
  • Multi-model routing for cost and capability
  • A system that improves instead of constantly starting over

How it works

Phase 1

Installation

Hardware is prepared, core software is installed, channels are connected, and the system is brought online in a stable private environment.

Phase 2

Personalization

Identity, memory, workflows, communication rules, and model routing are tuned around the actual person or household using the system.

Phase 3

Specialists

Focused agents are added where useful, such as idea strategy, mission control, cultural work, language learning, or property operations.

Phase 4

Stewardship

The system is maintained, tuned, and expanded over time so it grows into a real personal infrastructure layer rather than a static install.

The offer

Hardware, installation, configuration, and care.

Mini Mi is best understood as a managed system, not a login. The value comes from the dedicated machine, the initial setup, the tuning, the specialist layer, and the ongoing stewardship that makes it actually useful.

Not for

  • People who want a toy chatbot
  • People who want a generic SaaS AI app
  • People who do not want any setup or stewardship relationship

Simple thesis

Creator tools help you generate. Mini Mi is meant to help you continue. It is a private intelligence layer that belongs to you, remembers your world, and reduces the amount of life you have to carry manually.
